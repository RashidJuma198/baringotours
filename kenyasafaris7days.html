7 Days 6 Nights Kenya Safari Itinerary

  1. Seven Wonders of the World
  2. Explore the World!
Nairobi-Amboseli-Naivasha-Masai Mara
  • Day 1 Nairobi

    • Arrive at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport, You will Meet our representative, chauffeured to your city hotel for safari briefing and overnight

  • Day 2 Amboseli

    • Travel early in the morning from Nairobi into the Amboseli National Park's bushes for breathtaking vistas of Africa, Situated amidst undulating savannah landscapes, it is eclipsed by the pink-hued, snow-capped summit of Mount Kilimanjaro, A large herd of elephants roams the majestic slopes of Mount Kilimanjaro, Amboseli National Park is a haven for birdwatchers, home to more than 400 different kinds of birds, reptiles, plants, and animals, as well as some of Africa's most breathtaking vistas

    Take in some game viewing while traveling to get to your camp or lodge in time for lunch

    After some downtime, you'll go on an evening game watching drive to see wild creatures in their native surroundings

    After your nighttime game viewing drive, have dinner and spend the night at your camp or lodge

    Day 3 Amboseli

    Enjoy morning and afternoon game drives in the park on this day, or go on a full-day game drive with picnic lunch boxes

    In this magnificent setting, take pleasure in your quest for amazing animal encounters. If you're fortunate, you could be able to spot a lion, elephant, leopard, rhino, cheetah, buffalo, and a variety of plains game

    Go back to the hotel for the night and dinner

    Dinner and overnight at the lodge

  • Day 4 Naivasha

    • Early in the day After eating breakfast at the hotel, travel through Nairobi to Lake Naivasha while taking in the expansive, breathtaking scenery

    Freshwater Lake Naivasha is well-known for its large numbers of flamingos and hippos

    With more than 400 bird species calling the lake home, it is a birder's dream come true

    Get to your camp in time for lunch and check-in

    Spend the remainder of the day relaxing or taking advantage of Lake Naivasha's array of activities

    Dinner and overnight at the lodge

    Optional activities, Boat Trips, Bird watching and Nature Walks, and visit to Crescent Island

  • Day 5 Maasai Mara

    • Depart from Narok town for Maasai Mara after breakfast

    Unconstrained and free to roam, the Maasai Mara National Reserve is a unique wildlife conservation haven renowned for its amazing natural range of wildlife, It serves as a habitat to numerous animal species, including African buffalo, lions, cheetahs, elephants, rhinos, giraffes, and zebras, among many others

    While traveling, take in some game viewing and arrive at your lodge or camp in time for lunch and check-in

    You'll have time to unwind after lunch before going on a game drive in the evening, right before the animals rise from their afternoon nap

    Dinner and overnight at the lodge

  • Day 6 Maasai Mara

    • After eating breakfast at the camp/lodge, head out for a full day of game viewing with picnic lunch boxes, or take games in the morning and afternoon in the Masai Mara Game Reserve, stopping for lunch in between

    On this day, you can choose to take a hot-air balloon flight that begins early in the morning and ends with a Champagne breakfast on the plains, or you can visit a Masai village

    Dinner and overnight at the lodge

  • Day 7 Departure

    • After breakfast at the hotel, return to Nairobi.

    Our expert driver guide will drop you off at the airport hotel so you can check into your day room while you wait for your flight to your next location
