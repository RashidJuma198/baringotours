Explore Accommodation in the heart of Baringo!

Come and join us in experiencing the highest water levels in living memory!!

Approximately four hours drive from Nairobi in the heart of Kenyas Great Rift Valley, lies Lake Baringo and lake Bogoria - on the threshold of Kenya’s Northern Deserts.

Located in the heart of Marigat Town, 18km to lake Baringo and 21km to lake Bogoria. Riverside Homes AirBnB offers best accommodation facilities and meals.

Why chose Riverside Airbnb?

✅ Accessibility to Road: Riverside is located opposite to Marigat Bridge on Nakuru-Sigor Road.

✅ Clean Rooms and facilities: Our rooms are clean, have TVs, Kitchen, kitchen equipments, Wardrobe, Hot water showers and Air conditioners are available.

✅ WiFi availability

✅ Garden: Riverside Homes has a small garden and tables to relax when out of your room. Suitable for children to play and picnics as well.

✅ Meals: You can book for meals or cook for yourselves. This allows you to have a variety of options when it comes to your suitable choice of meal and Accommodation.

✅ Parking: Riverside Airbnb has a good parking space all kind of vehicles.

✅ Security: We have 24/7 security guards at the gate and CCTVs installed.

✅ Purified water: Riverside Homes Airbnb has clean and purified water inside the facility.

✅ Conference Room: We have a conference room that accomodate 50 people. Whether you are looking for a quiet place to hold a meeting, this is the place to be.

✅ Book for Other Activities:You can Book for Boat riding in lake Baringo, Birdwatching and other activities to do while in Baringo County.

ROOM TYPES AND RATES

✅One Bedroom: Ksh 2000 per night
