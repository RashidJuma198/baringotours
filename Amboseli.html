Amboseli National Park

Amboseli National Park is one of Kenya’s most popular parks in Kenya, located Loitoktok District, Rift Valley. The park is famous for being the best place in Africa to get close to free-ranging elephants among other wildlife species.

Other attraction of the park includes opportunities to meet the Maasai people and also offers spectacular views of Mount Kilimanjaro, the highest free-standing mountain in the world.

Accommodations

There is a wide selection of hotels in Amboseli ranging from Luxury, Mid-luxury to Budgeted hotels. We recommend staying 2 nights and add in a couple of other destinations too to your safari.

Amboseli National Park Safari Overview

Day 1: Nairobi – Amboseli National Park

Pick up from your Nairobi hotel or Airport in the morning. Early morning breakfast, After breakfast we drive to Amboseli National Park which is 5 Hrs drive.

Check in at Ol Tukai Lodge and have lunch. In the afternoon you will go for game drive in the park, the park offers some of the most magnificent game viewing in the world and attractions include : Mount Kilimanjaro offers scenic backgrounds for photography, elephant, rhino, Hippos, lions, cheetah, leopards, Buffalos, diverse plant and bird species. Later Dinner and overnight at the Ol Tukai lodge or a similar lodge.

Day 2: Amboseli National Park

Early morning breakfast, after breakfast check out with game drive in the Park.

You will see the Big Five animals such as Elephants, lions, Buffalos,leopards and cheetah among other animals found in the park.

After the game drive you will have lunch at the hotel and time to relax.

Later in the evening you will have cultural village tour to the maasai community.You will be entertained with traditional Maasai songs and dances as well as learning more about them by interacting.

Day 3: Amboseli – Nairobi

Early morning breakfast, thereafter we will leave to Nairobi and drop to your hotel or Airport.
