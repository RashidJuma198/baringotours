Boat Tours

We offer Boat riding in Lake Baringo. Our Boat ride comprises of variety of services which include:- Trip to Crocodiles and Hippopotamuses; Trip to Lake Baringo hotspring while in lake Baringo, Fish eagle feeding activity and Trip to Ruko conservancy. Our Boats are licensed, have a life safety jackets and guides. Book with us now and have an unforgettable experience. Boat charges is $30 per hour.

Game Drive Adventure Safaris

Enjoy Game drive adventure safaris to different game reserves, parks and conservancies in Kenya. Book a trip with Baringo Tours to Lake Baringo Conservancies, Lake Bogoria Game Reserve, Lake Nakuru National Park, Nairobi National Park, Masai Mara Game Reserve, Samburu, Tsavo East, Tsavo West and Amboseli National Park.

Camping in lake Baringo

We offer camping and hotel bookings in your specified destination. Whether you are looking for budgeted, mid-luxury or luxury accomodation, Baringo Tours is here to give you the best choice of hotels. We offer camping in lake Baringo for $15 per person.

Cultural Tours

Baringo Tours offers services to visit community villages where you will have a chance to experience traditional foods, artifacts, methods of food preservation, dressing and dances. You will be entertained with songs and dances while learning more about communities of your interest.Starting from the Maasai in Masai Mara and Amboseli, Pokot in Baringo and the Mijikenda in the Costal region of Kenya.

Birdwatching Safaris

Enjoy Birdwatching safaris to Important Bird Areas in Kenya. Our Team is determined to give you the best guides species of birds found in the region. Some Important Bird Area that has to offer include Baringo, Arabuko sokoke, Kakamega forest, Amboseli among many other places. Start your birdwatching journey today with us.

Travel Guides

We provide you with the best professional travel guide for your stay and travel in Kenya. Direct your queries to us and we will respond on time.

Educational Trips

Enjoy educational lectures about Lake Baringo, Lake Bogoria and Kenya at large from our experienced guides in the field of tourism and wildlife. University and secondary school students have been benefiting by learning their core units and subjects outside classroom
