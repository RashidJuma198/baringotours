3 Days 2 Nights in Masai Mara

  1. Seven Wonders of the World
  2. Explore the World!
Nairobi-Masai Mara
  • Day 1 Nairobi

    • Arrive at Jomo Kenyatta International Airport

    You will Meet our representative, chauffeured to your city hotel for breakfast.

    You will then proceed to Masai Mara in the afternoon.

    Enjoy a vast of interesting sightings, accompanied by a stop at the Great Rift Valley Viewpoint

    Enjoy a beautiful photo session as you continue with your drive to Masai Mara

    Enjoy a evening game viewing drive enroute to your camp/lodge arriving in time for dinner

  • Day 2 Maasai Mara

    • Enjoy an early breakfast at the hotel or Camp of your choice. Start morning game drive.

    The Maasai Mara National Reserve is a special place for wildlife conservation that is well-known for its incredible diversity of wildlife

    It is home to many different animal species, including lions, cheetahs, elephants, rhinos, African buffalo, wildebeests, giraffes, and zebras, all of which are free to roam and live in their natural habitat

    Enjoy a drive to see the nighttime wildlife after lunch and get back to your camp or lodge in time for lunch

    Dinner and overnight at the lodge

    Day 3 Departure

    Enjoy a wildlife watching drive while departing the Maasai Mara Reserve and returning to Nairobi after an early breakfast

    In order to give you time to get ready for your international departure flight to your next location, our experienced driver guide will drop you off at your hotel for lunch and a day room
