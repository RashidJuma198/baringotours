This 3-day safari starts in Nairobi, with a scenic drive to the Maasai Mara, stopping at the Great Rift Valley. Explore the diverse wildlife of the Maasai Mara, including lions, elephants, and cheetahs, through game drives. Optional activities include hot-air balloon rides or visiting a Maasai village. The adventure concludes with a return to Nairobi. Facility: Nairobi: Hotel, Outside Nairobi: Lodge Accommodation type: 2Pax Room type: double room Duration: 3 days, 2 nights.
