Our on-site Services

Boat Rides

Our Boat ride comprises of variety of services which include:- Trip to Crocodiles and Hippopotamuses; Full ride that comprises a joy ride: Trip to Lake Baringo hotspring; Trip to Devil's Island; Fish eagle feeding activity and Trip to Ruko conservancy. Our Boats are licensed, have a life safety jackets and guides. Book with us now and have an unforgettable experience.

Bird Watching

Enjoy bird watching while in lake Baringo. Our Team is determined to give you the best guides on more than 400 species of birds found in Lake Baringo conservation region. You can also book for bird watching activity in Kakamega forest with us.

Cultural village visits

Baringo is rich in culture from different communities. Baringo Tours offers services to visit community villages where you will have a chance to experience traditional foods, artifacts, methods of food preservation, dressing and dances. You will be entertained with songs and dances while learning more about communities around here.

Guided nature walk and Hiking

Experience the glamorous nature starting wild trees and shrubs, birds, hilly escarpments and Lake view point, Carpet viper snake,Red scorpion and Black scorpion,. Enjoy Hiking experience in the lake Baringo Cliffs and Morop hills.

Educational Trip Lectures

Enjoy educational lectures about Lake Baringo, Lake Bogoria and Kenya at large from our experienced guides in the field of tourism and wildlife. University and secondary school students have been benefiting by learning their core units and subjects outside classroom.

Game Drive Safaris

Enjoy Game drive adventure safaris to different game reserves, parks and conservancies in Kenya. Book a trip with Baringo Tours to Lake Baringo Conservancies, Lake Bogaoria Game Reserve, Lake Nakuru National Park, Nairobi National Park, Masai Mara Game Reserve, Samburu and Amboseli Game Reserve.
